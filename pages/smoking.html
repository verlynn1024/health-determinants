Smoking Rates in Europe

Overview

Smoking remains a significant health concern across Europe, with varying prevalence rates and trends among different countries.

Health Impacts

  • Lung cancer
  • Cardiovascular diseases
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Increased risk of other cancers

Tobacco Control Measures

European countries have implemented various tobacco control policies, including:

  • Smoking bans in public places
  • Higher taxation on tobacco products
  • Plain packaging requirements
  • Advertising restrictions
