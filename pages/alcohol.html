Alcohol Consumption in Europe

Overview

Alcohol consumption is a significant health determinant across Europe, with varying patterns and impacts among different countries.

Alcohol Consumption Trends

Country Comparison

Health Impacts

  • Liver disease
  • Cardiovascular issues
  • Mental health problems
  • Increased risk of injuries

Policy Measures

Various European countries have implemented policies to reduce alcohol consumption, including:

  • Pricing policies
  • Advertising restrictions
  • Age limits for purchasing alcohol
  • Drink-driving countermeasures
